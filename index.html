Xiuheng Wang

PhD Candidate at Université Côte d'Azur

Parc Valrose, 06108 Nice cedex 2 - France.

I am a PhD Candidate in Electrical Engineering at Laboratoire Lagrange of Université Côte d’Azur, under the supervision of Professor Cédric Richard. I also work with CNRS Researher Ricardo Borsoi.

Before that, I received my master degree in 2021 under the supervision of Professor Jie Chen, from Northwestern Polytechnical University, Xi’an, China, where I also received my bachelor degree in 2018.

My recent research interests focus on optimization on manifolds, change point detection, and hyperspectral image analysis. A formal biography as well as academic CV are available here.

I am currently on the job market.
